Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our outbound meeting generation system.

What makes MZ TECHNOLOGIES different? +

Unlike traditional agencies, we run a fully-managed 3-channel outbound system — LinkedIn, Email, and Instagram DM. You get qualified meetings booked directly into your calendar, not just raw leads to chase.

Which industries do you work with? +

We specialise in Financial Services, Real Estate, Healthcare, Marketing Agencies, SaaS, Consulting, and Coaching — industries where high-value appointments directly translate into revenue.

How quickly can I expect results? +

Most founders start seeing qualified meetings booked within the first 7–14 days of launching their outbound campaign.

What channels do you use for outreach? +

We use a proven 3-channel system: LinkedIn outreach sequences, cold email campaigns, and Instagram DM outreach — all backed by follow-up automation so no prospect goes cold.

Do you handle appointment booking too? +

Yes. Qualified prospects are booked directly into your calendar. You also receive monthly reporting with full transparency on outreach performance, reply rates, and meetings generated.

Is there a long-term contract requirement? +

We believe in results over long contracts. While we recommend at least 3 months to see the full scaling effect, we offer flexible month-to-month plans.
